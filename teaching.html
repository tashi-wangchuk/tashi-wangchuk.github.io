Here is a collection of lecture notes, problem sheets, and other material for physics for different grades. The courses are taught at The Royal Academy. Corrections and suggestions are always welcome at tashiwangchuk@academy.bt.

Fundamentals of Physics

Grade 11 · The Royal Academy, Druk Gyalpo's Institute, Pangbisa, Paro · 2026

Notes for my Grade 11 physics course. They are anything but original: my main contribution has been to gather, adapt, and weave together the clearest discussions and explanations I could find in the vast literature, with the emphasis on building physical intuition before formalism. The notes are very much a work in progress — they grow and improve as the course unfolds.
