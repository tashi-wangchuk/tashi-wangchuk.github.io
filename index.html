I am a teacher-researcher at Druk Gyalpo's Institute, but at heart, I am a physicist.

I did my undergrad in Math-Physics at the University of New Brunswick in Canada. While there, I was fortunate to receive the Dr. Margot Roach Scholarship and work at the MRI Research Centre under Bruce Balcom. You can find my CV here.

Lately, quantum information theory, quantum computing, and quantum error correction have taken hold of my attention, and I hope to go much deeper into them in the years ahead. The Royal Academy Observatory here at the Institute, part of the PANOPTES network, pulled me into astronomy almost by accident. I'd like to build something good there with my students.

I live in Pangbisa, Paro. Outside of work, you'll find me reading, at the gym, or watching a good film.
